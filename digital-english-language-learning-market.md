<p><p>The Digital English Language Learning market consists of two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. In contrast, cloud-based solutions offer flexibility and accessibility via the internet, allowing users to learn from any device without extensive infrastructure. This model typically features lower upfront costs and easier updates, making it popular among individual learners and educational institutions.</p></p>
