<p><strong>The Data Leakage Prevention Product Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Host based DLP</li><li>Network based DLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data Leakage Prevention (DLP) products are essential for safeguarding sensitive information within organizations. Host-based DLP focuses on protecting data residing on individual devices, preventing unauthorized access and sharing by monitoring and controlling data flows locally. In contrast, network-based DLP monitors and protects data as it travels across the network, using techniques such as deep packet inspection to identify and block potential data breaches. Both types work together to create a comprehensive security strategy against data loss.</p></p>
