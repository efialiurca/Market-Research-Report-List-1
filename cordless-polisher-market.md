<p><p>The cordless polisher market is primarily segmented into two types: rotary cordless polishers and dual-action cordless polishers. Rotary cordless polishers utilize a single circular motion, generating high speed and power for intensive polishing tasks, making them ideal for experienced users and professional applications. In contrast, dual-action cordless polishers combine rotational and oscillating movements, offering a gentler polishing experience that minimizes the risk of paint damage, making them suitable for beginners and enthusiasts seeking a versatile and user-friendly tool.</p></p>
