<p><p>The High Voltage Offshore Cables market consists primarily of HVDC (High Voltage Direct Current) and HVAC (High Voltage Alternating Current) cables. HVDC cables are designed for efficient long-distance power transmission with lower energy losses and can connect asynchronous grids. In contrast, HVAC cables facilitate shorter-distance transmission, commonly used for offshore wind farms, enabling the integration of renewable energy sources to onshore grids. Both cable types are essential for enhancing energy security and optimizing the offshore energy infrastructure.</p></p>
