<p><p>The mobile ventilator market is categorized into three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. </p><p>Electronic ventilators utilize complex circuitry and software for advanced patient monitoring and synchronization, allowing for precise control over breathing parameters. Pneumatic ventilators rely on compressed air to operate, offering simplicity and reliability, often in emergency or transport situations. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both technologies, providing flexibility and enhanced functionality, making them suitable for various clinical settings where both portability and performance are crucial.</p></p>
