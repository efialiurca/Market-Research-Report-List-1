<p><p>The insulating glass market comprises various types, including Conventional Insulating Glass Units (IGUs), which typically feature two or more panes sealed with a spacer, providing basic thermal performance. Low-E Insulating Glass Units incorporate a thin, transparent coating that reflects infrared light, enhancing energy efficiency. Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units feature a reflective coating that minimizes solar heat gain while maintaining aesthetics. Each type serves distinct needs in energy conservation, comfort, and design in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
