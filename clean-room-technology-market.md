<p><p>Clean Room Technology encompasses various market types essential for maintaining controlled environments. Fan Filter Units (FFUs) provide localized air filtration, while HVAC Systems regulate temperature and humidity. Laminar Air Flow Systems ensure unidirectional airflow to prevent contamination, and Biosafety Cabinets protect users while handling hazardous materials. HEPA Filters efficiently trap airborne particles, and Air Diffusers distribute clean air evenly. Showers are designed for personnel decontamination before entering sterile zones, ensuring compliance with stringent cleanliness standards in industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
