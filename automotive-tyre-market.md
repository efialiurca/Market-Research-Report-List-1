<p><p>The automotive tyre market is divided into two main segments: Original Equipment (OE) tyres and Replacement tyres. OE tyres are those supplied by manufacturers and fitted to vehicles during production, designed to meet specific performance standards. Replacement tyres, on the other hand, are purchased by consumers to replace worn-out or damaged tyres after the vehicle has been sold. Each segment serves distinct needs, with OE tyres focusing on initial quality and performance, while replacement tyres prioritize longevity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
