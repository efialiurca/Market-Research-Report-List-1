<p><strong>The Wireless ANC Headphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Cup (Circumaural)</li><li>On-Ear (Supraaural)</li><li>In-Ear (Intraaural)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Active Noise Cancelling (ANC) headphone market features three main types: Full Cup (Circumaural), On-Ear (Supraaural), and In-Ear (Intraaural) designs. Full Cup headphones encompass the entire ear, providing superior noise isolation and comfort, ideal for immersive listening. On-Ear models rest directly on the ears, balancing portability and sound quality but may allow some ambient noise. In-Ear headphones fit snugly within the ear canal, offering a compact design and effective noise cancellation, making them popular for active lifestyles.</p></p>
