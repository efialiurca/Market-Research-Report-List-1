<p><p>The 3D modeling market encompasses various techniques, primarily NURBS (Non-Uniform Rational B-Splines) and Polygon Mesh. NURBS are used for creating smooth, mathematically defined surfaces, ideal for automotive and industrial design due to their precision and flexibility. Conversely, Polygon Mesh models are composed of vertices, edges, and faces, making them suitable for real-time applications like gaming and animation. Both methods cater to different needs in industries such as entertainment, architecture, and product design, enhancing creativity and technical accuracy.</p></p>
