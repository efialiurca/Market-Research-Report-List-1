<p><strong>The Atomized Brass Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Atomization</li><li>Gas Atomization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomized brass powder market is categorized primarily into two types: water atomization and gas atomization. Water atomization involves using high-pressure water jets to break molten brass into fine droplets, resulting in a more irregular particle shape and a lower production cost. In contrast, gas atomization utilizes inert gas streams to produce spherical particles, offering superior flowability and packing density. Both methods serve various applications in industries like additive manufacturing, electronics, and automotive components, each with unique benefits and characteristics.</p></p>
