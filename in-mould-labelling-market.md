<p><strong>The In Mould Labelling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion- Blow Molding</li><li>Thermoforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-mould labeling (IML) encompasses various molding techniques for integrating labels directly into plastic products during their manufacturing process. Injection molding involves injecting molten plastic into a mold, allowing for precise, high-volume production of items like containers and lids with embedded labels. Extrusion-blow molding combines extrusion and blowing to create hollow items, effectively incorporating labels during formation. Thermoforming uses heat to mold plastic sheets over labels, producing customized packaging solutions. Each method benefits from IML by enhancing aesthetics and functionality while reducing post-production labeling costs.</p></p>
