<p><p>Tree pruning services encompass three primary market types: thinning, raising, and reduction. Thinning involves selectively removing branches to improve light penetration and air circulation, promoting healthier growth. Raising focuses on removing lower branches to increase clearance, enhancing visibility or access beneath trees. Reduction pruning shortens the overall height or spread of a tree while maintaining its shape, helping to manage size and reduce risks associated with overgrown trees. Each type addresses specific tree health and aesthetic needs.</p></p>
