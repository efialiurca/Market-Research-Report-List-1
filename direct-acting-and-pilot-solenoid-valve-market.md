<p><p>The solenoid valve market consists of three main types: Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves, Sub-Step Direct-Acting Valves, and Pilot Solenoid Valves. Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves operate directly on the fluid flow with the solenoid actuator, offering swift response times and compact designs. Sub-Step Direct-Acting Valves provide intermediary control for more precise flow management. Pilot Solenoid Valves, on the other hand, use a smaller solenoid to control a larger flow, enhancing efficiency in complex systems, particularly where space is limited.</p></p>
