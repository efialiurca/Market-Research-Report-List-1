<p><p>The electric energy metering analog front end market is categorized into simplex and polyphase types. Simplex meters measure energy in single-phase systems, commonly used in residential settings, where one voltage and current channel is sufficient. Polyphase meters, on the other hand, are designed for industrial and commercial applications, measuring energy across multiple phases, typically three-phase systems. This differentiation allows for tailored solutions that meet the diverse energy monitoring needs, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in energy consumption tracking.</p></p>
