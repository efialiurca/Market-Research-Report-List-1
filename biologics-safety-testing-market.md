<p><p>The Biologics Safety Testing Market encompasses various testing types to ensure the safety and efficacy of biological products. Endotoxin tests detect harmful bacterial byproducts, while sterility tests assess the absence of microbes. Cell line authentication and characterization tests verify the identity and quality of cell lines used in production. Bioburden tests measure the microbial load, while residual host contaminant detection tests identify any leftover substances from the host cells. Adventitious agent detection tests screen for unintended pathogens, supplemented by others for specialized assessments.</p></p>
