<p><p>The electric ferry market is segmented into two main types: pure electric and hybrid power ferries. Pure electric ferries run solely on battery power, offering zero emissions and reduced operational costs but requiring significant charging infrastructure. In contrast, hybrid power ferries utilize both electric power and traditional fuels, allowing for greater range and flexibility while still lowering emissions compared to conventional vessels. Both types aim to enhance sustainability and improve marine transportation efficiency in response to environmental concerns.</p></p>
