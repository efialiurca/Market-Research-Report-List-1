<p><p>Plastic injection molding services encompass various market types, including horizontal molding, insert molding, overmolding, and others. Horizontal molding is the most common, using a horizontal clamp to inject molten plastic into a mold. Insert molding involves placing components, like metal inserts, into the mold before the plastic is injected. Overmolding combines two materials, typically involving a soft outer layer over a rigid core for enhanced functionality. Other methods may include multi-material or micro-molding to address specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
