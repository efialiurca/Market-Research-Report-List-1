<p><p>The salon-use hair coloring products market consists of three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes offer short-lived color, typically lasting a few washes, making them ideal for experimenting or special occasions. Semi-permanent dyes provide longer-lasting color, generally fading after several washes, and can enhance natural shades. In contrast, permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft for long-lasting results, effectively covering greys and allowing for significant color changes. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
