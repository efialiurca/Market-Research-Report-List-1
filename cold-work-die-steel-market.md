<p><p>The Cold Work Die Steel market includes various applications essential for metalworking processes. Blanking dies are used for cutting shapes from sheet metal, while cold upsetting dies shape materials through compression. Cold extrusion dies facilitate the shaping of metals by forcing them through a die, enhancing strength and precision. Bending dies are utilized to form metal into specific angles, while drawing dies help in elongating metal into desired profiles. Each type plays a crucial role in efficient and precise metal fabrication.</p></p>
