<p><p>The automotive map light market is segmented into three primary types: LED, halogen, and xenon. LED lights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and produce bright illumination with minimal heat, making them popular in modern vehicles. Halogen lights offer a warm light output and are relatively inexpensive, but consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. Xenon lights, known for their high-intensity discharge, provide bright, clear light that enhances visibility but are generally more expensive and require specialized installation. Each type offers unique benefits to meet various consumer preferences.</p></p>
