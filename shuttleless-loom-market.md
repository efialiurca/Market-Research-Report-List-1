<p><strong>The Shuttleless Loom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapier (Single and Double Type)</li><li>Air Jet Loom</li><li>Water Jet Loom</li><li>Gripper or Projectile Loom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shuttleless loom market comprises various types, including rapier looms, which use a flexible carrier to insert the weft yarn, available in single and double types for different production needs. Air jet looms utilize compressed air to propel the weft, offering high-speed weaving. Water jet looms employ high-pressure water to insert the weft yarn, suitable for specific fabrics. Gripper or projectile looms utilize a gripper to carry the weft, providing efficient weaving for heavy fabrics. Each type serves distinct textile manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
