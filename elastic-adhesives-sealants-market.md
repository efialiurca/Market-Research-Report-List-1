<p><p>The elastic adhesives and sealants market comprises three primary types: polyurethane, silicone, and SMP (Silyl Modified Polymer). Polyurethane adhesives offer excellent flexibility and durability, making them ideal for construction and automotive applications. Silicone sealants are known for their high temperature resistance and weatherproof properties, suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. SMP combines the best features of both polyurethane and silicone, providing strong adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors, making it versatile for various applications, including bonding and sealing.</p></p>
