<p><p>The Pressure Infusion Devices market is divided into two main types: Manual Pressure Infusors and Automatic Pressure Infusors. Manual Pressure Infusors require healthcare professionals to manually apply pressure to the infuser bag to deliver fluids, offering portability and simplicity. In contrast, Automatic Pressure Infusors utilize automated systems to maintain consistent pressure, enhancing precision and reducing the risk of human error. Both types are essential for rapid fluid administration, especially in emergency and critical care settings, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
