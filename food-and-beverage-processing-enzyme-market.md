<p><p>The food and beverage processing enzyme market includes three main types: carbohydrases, proteases, and lipases. Carbohydrases are used to break down complex carbohydrates, enhancing sweetness, texture, and fermentation in products like bread and beverages. Proteases aid in protein breakdown, improving digestibility and flavor in dairy and meat products. Lipases catalyze the breakdown of fats, enhancing flavor and texture in dairy, snacks, and oils. Together, these enzymes play a crucial role in optimizing food quality and production efficiency.</p></p>
