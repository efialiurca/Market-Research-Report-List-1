<p><ul><li>Total RNA</li><li>Pre-mRNA</li><li>Noncoding RNA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RNA sequencing analysis encompasses several market types based on the RNA species being studied. Total RNA involves sequencing all RNA present in a sample, providing a comprehensive view of gene expression. Pre-mRNA focuses on the precursor transcripts before they are processed into mature mRNA, offering insights into transcriptional regulation. Noncoding RNA targets RNA molecules that do not translate into proteins but play critical roles in gene expression regulation, cellular processes, and maintaining genome stability. These segments cater to diverse research and clinical applications.</p></p>
