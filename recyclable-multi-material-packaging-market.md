Ethylene vinyl alcohol (EVOH) enhances oxygen barrier performance, crucial for preserving product freshness. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) is favored for its recyclability and strength. Other materials may include biodegradable options and innovative composites, catering to diverse packaging needs and sustainability goals.</p></p>
