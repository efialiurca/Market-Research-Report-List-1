<p><p>The drug delivery technology market encompasses various administration routes, including oral, injectable, topical, ocular, nasal, pulmonary, transmucosal, and implantable. Oral delivery is the most common method, utilizing tablets and capsules. Injectable systems provide rapid effects, ranging from vaccines to biologics. Topical delivery targets localized effects through creams and gels. Ocular delivery focuses on eye conditions, while nasal and pulmonary methods enable rapid systemic absorption. Transmucosal delivery utilizes mucous membranes for medication absorption, and implantable devices offer sustained release over time.</p></p>
