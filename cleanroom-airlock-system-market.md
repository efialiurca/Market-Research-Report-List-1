<p><p>The Cleanroom Airlock System market comprises various types designed to maintain sterile environments. Cascading airlocks use a sequential entry process to control airflow and prevent contamination. Bubble airlocks utilize a pressurized chamber that creates a "bubble" of air, ensuring contaminants are kept out. Sink airlocks are designed to incorporate handwashing and equipment sanitization, enhancing cleanliness during entry. Other types may include customized solutions tailored to specific industry needs, ensuring optimal control over environmental conditions.</p></p>
