<p><p>The electric power fitting market encompasses various components that ensure the effective transmission and distribution of electricity. Suspension clamps secure conductors to poles or towers, preventing sagging. Strain clamps manage mechanical tension, especially at cable terminations. Link fittings connect conductors and facilitate electrical connections. Protective fittings safeguard wires and cables from environmental damage and wear. Additionally, the "others" category includes specialized fittings used for unique applications in the electric power sector, contributing to system reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
