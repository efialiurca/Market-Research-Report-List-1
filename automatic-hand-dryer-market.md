<p><ul><li>Hot Air Dryer</li><li>Jet Air Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic hand dryer market features two primary types: hot air dryers and jet air dryers. Hot air dryers use a steady stream of warm air to evaporate moisture from hands, typically taking longer to dry but offering a quieter operation. In contrast, jet air dryers utilize high-velocity air jets, providing rapid drying in a shorter time. Both types are designed for high-traffic areas, promoting hygiene and reducing paper towel waste, catering to varying preferences for speed and energy efficiency.</p></p>
