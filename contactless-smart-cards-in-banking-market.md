<p><p>Contactless smart cards in the banking market utilize Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC) technologies to enable secure, swift transactions without physical contact. RFID allows the card to communicate with readers using electromagnetic fields, while RFIC integrates the communication and processing capabilities within the card. These technologies enhance user convenience by facilitating tap-and-go payments, improving transaction speed, and maintaining security through encryption, making them increasingly popular in digital banking solutions and payment systems.</p></p>
