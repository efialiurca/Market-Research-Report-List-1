<p><p>The household coffee machine market encompasses three main types: drip coffee machines, capsule coffee machines, and pressure coffee machines. Drip coffee machines brew coffee by dripping hot water over ground coffee, offering versatility and volume. Capsule coffee machines utilize pre-packaged coffee pods for convenience and consistency, appealing to busy consumers. Pressure coffee machines, commonly known as espresso machines, use high pressure to extract rich flavors, catering to coffee enthusiasts who seek a robust beverage experience. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences.</p></p>
