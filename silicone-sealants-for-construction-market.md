<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone sealants for construction are categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component sealants are ready to use and cure through moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. They are ideal for sealing joints and gaps. In contrast, two-component sealants consist of a base and a hardener that are mixed before application. This approach offers enhanced durability and adhesion, making them suitable for demanding environments where higher performance is required.</p></p>
