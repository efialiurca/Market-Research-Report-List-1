<p><p>The compostable cup market consists of various types, primarily classified into paper-coated products and thermoforming products. On the other hand, thermoforming products are created through heating and molding bioplastics, resulting in lightweight and durable designs suitable for various uses. Both types focus on reducing environmental impact while providing functional, sustainable options in the growing market for compostable food service items.</p></p>
