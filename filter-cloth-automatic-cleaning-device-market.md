<p><p>The filter cloth automatic cleaning device market includes three distinct types: Single Shot Flush, Double Lever Flush, and Rolling Scrub. </p><p>Single Shot Flush utilizes a single burst of water to dislodge debris, ensuring quick cleaning with minimal water use. Double Lever Flush offers a more thorough cleaning through multiple flush cycles, enhancing efficiency for heavier loads. Rolling Scrub employs a mechanical scrubber that rotates across the filter cloth, providing an effective deep clean suitable for demanding applications. Each type caters to varying filtration needs and operational environments.</p></p>
